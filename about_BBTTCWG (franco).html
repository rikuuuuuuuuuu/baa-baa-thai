Baa Baa Thai Tea Collaborates with Ghirardelli

Baa Baa Thai Tea’s newest collection of frappes is here! In collaboration with the world-renowned Ghirardelli Chocolate Company, we’re bringing together the best of both worlds; Baa Baa’s unique flavors and the rich, luxurious taste of Ghirardelli chocolate. As two beloved brands come together, we promise our customers an unforgettable and irresistible experience with these #LuxuriousTreats

Starting May 17, 2024, we will launch our eight new frappe collections! With a variety of espresso or non-espresso frappes to choose from: Dark Caramel Coffee, Dark Chocolate Chips, Sea Salt Caramel Milk Chocolate, Biscoff Milk Chocolate, Vanilla Cream, Milk Chocolate & Espresso, White Chocolate & Espresso, and Dark Chocolate & Espresso. All for you to enjoy!

Baa Baa Thai Tea’s partnership with Ghirardelli Chocolate Company represents a bold step forward in beverage innovation. Each frappe is meticulously crafted to provide an unparalleled taste experience that will delight and satisfy our customers as we bring our chocolate indulgence to the next level.

Visit Baa Baa Thai Tea today and taste the luxury!
